As Seen in the Movies!

1/10/1989

Julie said "some guy in a Hawaiian shirt is here to see you". Imagine my surprise when I got to the counter and it was Mr. Weird Al Yankovick! I am a big Weird Al fan. It is a great honor for Spatula City to be in a scene in his upcoming movie titled UHF.

Julie and I stayed up all night shining the spatulas and cleaning the parking lot. I am nervous about my quick interview at the end of the scene. I will try to memorize the lines, but I'll probably end up reading them.

An Electronic Spatula?

10/18/1985

Some stiff-neck salesman tried to sell us a line of Electronic spatulas today. He pulled out a thick spatula with buttons and knobs all over it and told me it would be the latest craze in kitchen automation. He left me the sample, but I am afraid to turn it on.
